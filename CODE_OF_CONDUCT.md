# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Contributors should communicate clearly, respectfully, and in good faith. The
project welcomes direct technical critique, but not harassment, personal attacks,
or discriminatory behavior.

## Expected Behavior

- Be specific when reporting bugs or review concerns.
- Assume technical disagreement is about the work, not the person.
- Respect privacy and do not post secrets, private traces, or sensitive repo data.
- Keep discussions focused on making the middleware useful and trustworthy.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private security reports before maintainers can respond.
- Sharing real API keys, private customer data, or non-public source material.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may moderate issues, pull requests, and discussions to protect the
project and contributors. Serious or repeated violations may result in blocked
participation.
